# Research Context: AI in Social Sciences

## Understanding the Domain
The intersection of AI and social sciences explores how algorithms impact social structures, individual behaviors, labor markets, and democratic institutions. We do not focus on the technical code, but on the societal outcomes and theoretical implications.

## Main Thematic Axes (Expected Clusters)
When analyzing the texts, pay special attention to the following streams:
1. **Ethics and Bias:** Algorithmic discrimination, reproduction of social inequalities.
2. **Work and Economy:** Automation, the gig economy, reskilling, the digital divide.
3. **Politics and Media:** Disinformation, deepfakes, filter bubbles, impact on elections.
4. **Relationships and Psychology:** Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), anthropomorphization of AI.

## Goal of the Analysis
We want to map the intellectual battlefield—who agrees with whom, what theories dominate, where the internal tensions lie, and where empirical evidence is lacking.
